In a world full of noise,
I found my calm.

Some moments don’t ask for explanations.
They just stay — quietly, warmly.

The first time I saw you,
my heart forgot how to stay calm.

I blushed… trying to hide it,
but you noticed anyway.

Somehow, that day,
you became my Valentine.

With a smile full of courage,
you believed in “us” — forever.

And today, I promise you my life,
for all the ages to come.

Three moments.
All quietly unforgettable.

I didn’t come here with a plan.
Just wondering… will “forever” still choose us?
